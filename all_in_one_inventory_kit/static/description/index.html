Overview

This module includes the following features, Export Product Stock in Excel, Multi Branch Operations, Product Brand in Inventory, Barcode scanning in Inventory, Inventory Dashboard Odoo 16, Stock Picking From Invoice, Inventory All In One Report Generator, Product Stock Details, Invoice From Stock Picking , Picking Line Description(Shipment/Delivery) and Catch Weight - Stock and Picking Order Line Views in a single module.

Features

Inventory Dashboard with all necessary details
Current Stock Report for all Products in each Warehouse
Add Brands to products
Avoid manual entry of item count in Stock Picking and Use barcode to add product
Invoice From Stock Picking
All In One Report Generator
Get product quantity reviews for each stock location from the product form and Print PDF report of them
Stock Picking From Customer Invoice and Stock Picking From Supplier bill
Order Line Description of Shipment and Delivery
Catch Weight of Stock
Incoming and Outgoing Picking Operations Views are included with images of their related products
Form, Tree, Kanban, Pivot, Graph and Calendar views of Incoming and Outgoing Picking Operations

Report

Go to Inventory -> Reporting -> Current stock in Excel. Now a wizard will appear on your screen. Please enter the warehouses which you want to take the report. You can also select category for products(It is Optional). Then Click "Export Product with Stock Info" button. Then You will get the corresponding report in XLSX.

Per warehouse, you can get Available Qty, Virtual Qty, Incoming Qty, Outgoing Qty, Net On Hand Qty, Total Sold & Total Purchased Qty. You can get your stock valuation details too. Negative stock will be highlighted in "red" cells

Product Brand

Navigate into Product-->Product Brands to Product Brand Menu.

Create a new Product Brand . We can add products directly from here itself.

Also, we can add the brand inside the product form.

Group the products by Product Brand.

Barcode to add Product

Enable the edit mode, Click the field 'Barcode' and scan the product. Now you can see the quantity is automatically Updated.

If no associated product found in list then a warning is popup.
